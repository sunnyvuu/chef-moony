Chicken and Rice

Description

Here's our easy-to-cook recipe of homemade cat food with chicken and rice!
This recipe produces cat food that your kitty will love.

Ingredients

  • 85 g baked dark chicken meat
  • 50 g boiled white rice
  • 30 g boiled potato
  • 4 g olive oil

Directions

  1. Mix all these ingredients together and the meal is ready.
  2. Store the leftovers in your airtight container in the refrigerator.
